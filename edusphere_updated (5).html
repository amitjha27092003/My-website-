🚀 STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE
  1. 1 📸 Topic image ya screenshot upload karo — textbook page, question paper, diagram — kuch bhi
  2. 2 ✏️ Ya seedha topic text type karo — quick chips se bhi choose kar sakte ho
  3. 3 🌐 Language choose karo — top-right ke globe icon se (Hinglish, Hindi, English)
  4. 4 🎯 Exam type select karo — Class 9-12, JEE, NEET, UPSC, College
  5. 5 🎬 Generate Explainer click karo — Jennie AI 8 scenes ready karegi
  6. 6 ▶️ Play karo — Ultra-HD animated scenes + Jennie ki Indian voice mein explanation suno!
